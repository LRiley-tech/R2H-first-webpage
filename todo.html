Tokyo National Museum

Description

If you are looking for a place to learn more about the history of Tokyo? Well this is the place for you. Explore some of the best art and archeological artifacts in Japan.

Admission:

  • Adult~ $5.67/620 yen
  • University Students~ $3.75/410 yen
  • 18 and under and over 70~ Free

Target age group:

  • Suits all ages
