IT

With many companies to choose from when it comes to software development; Tokyo is one of the top places to work when it comes to software development. Let explore some of the best IT companies in Tokyo, Japan.

Sony

Description:

Sony, one of the most popular companies worldwide, is headquarted in Kōnan, Minato, Tokyo. The company is know for its diverse business which include gaming, entertainment, financial services, etc. The company offers a variety of benefits and offers many diverse jobs, making it an appealing place to work.

Benefits:

  • 401k plan
  • Paid Holidays/Vacations
  • Paid sick leave
  • Education/Training/Tuition/Certification Reimbursement

Hitachi

Description:

Founded in 1910, hi mean sun and tachi meaning rise; the company is most know for its part in automotive systems and engineering. Hitachi was mostly recently in talks to merge with Honda. Hitachi offers many jobs including jobs in engineering, information technology, sales, etc. Hatachi as many tech companies has created a culture that embodies the improvement of others and working as a team.

Benefits:

  • 401k plan
  • Paid Holidays/Vacations
  • Paid sick leave
  • Life insurance

NTT Data Services

Description:

NTT Services was found in 1987 in Kōtō, Tokyo, Japan. NTT has many industries that include life sciences, manufacturing, retail & CPG, etc. Customer service is valued in this company. NTT strives to always be one step of customers and address their technical needs with out any issues. Their customer first moto sums up what this IT company is all about.

Benefits:

  • 401k plan
  • Paid Holidays
  • Dental Insurance
  • Work From Home Policy
